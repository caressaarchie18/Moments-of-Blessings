Marriage Ministry

God fashioned the body of Christ after the structure that he ordained in marriage. The Word of God tells us that marriage is honorable in the sight of God and the bed is undefiled. He also said that he that findeth a wife, findeth a good thing and obtains favor from the Lord. Many people have tried to alter the word of God, but His Word is true and shall stand forever.

As the husband is the head of the home, so is Christ the head of the church. [Ephesians 5:22-24]

22 Wives should submit to their husbands as they do to the Lord;23 because the husband is head of the wife, just as the Messiah, as head of the Messianic Community, is himself the one who keeps the body safe. 24 Just as the Messianic Community submits to the Messiah, so also wives should submit to their husbands in everything.

The Kingdom of Heaven towards the church is fashioned metaphorically as a marriage. When He returns, He called himself the bridegroom coming back for his bride. Revelation 21:2 (CJB) 2Also I saw the holy city, New Yerushalayim, coming down out of heaven from God, prepared like a bride beautifully dressed for her husband.

We believe in marriage and we believe in strong family units. For more than 25 years, every January during the last weekend of the month, couples escaped to the Central California Coast for The Annual Into-Mate Couple's Marriage Retreat. It was a time where couples came together for fellowship, fun, relaxation, spiritual rejuvenation, and marital strengthening.

We will always celebrate holy matrimony as God has designed it and look forward to God's favor showering down on all of the families that were blessed to be a part of such an awesome experience. Couples are encouraged to continue carving out special time for one another to keep romance alive. Life can take its toll on the best of marriages, but keeping it Christ focused and spouse centered will help you navigate even through the roughest peaks and valleys. Apostle quite frequently admonished all couples, "Be good to yourself and each other."

Music Ministry

Music is an important part of our corporate worship. Blessed with anointed musicians, praise and worship leaders and spirit-filled believers, God has anointed us to "raise the praise" and worship Him in the beauty of Holiness. We believe in giving everything we've got in total adoration for who He is and what He has done for us.

Because of His love, His compassion, and His tender mercies, we are not consumed. He is faithful to His Word. We are not ashamed to make a joyful noise unto the Lord. Praise is who we are and what we do. Our unique style of worship has something for everyone. We can reach back and bring the foot stomping "Song Service" that carried us through many decades to more modern contemporary sounds of worship. We focus on the message of the songs as we endeavor to lift of Jesus and not dwell on our problems and pain. It is through losing sight on what we battle or go through daily and yielding completely to the power and presence of God that we find deliverance. We offer up the sacrifice of praise which is our reasonable service.
