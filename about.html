About Moments of Blessings

Over 47 years ago, in a home on the west side of Fresno, a small group of people began to assemble on a regular basis to share and affirm one another in their spiritual and moral beliefs. As the group began to increase in size, they looked for a place to worship. After much searching, in July of 1976 one of our members happened upon a little church located at 922 S. Chance Avenue (formerly Eastgate Church of God) in the cities southeast corridor.

Along with the new location, came the responsibility of meeting the needs of those around us. To meet the demands of our community, the Moments of Blessings Church embarked on a partnership with the Community Food Bank, as a distribution site to provide food and other necessities to our neighbors in need. Recognizing the need to feed our children during the summer, the church began a summer lunch program for neighborhood children, there are youth outreach programs, a Prison ministry, a women's program (WIN-Women in Need) that targets all women from every walk of life as well as a Men's outreach. Realizing that charity begins at home then spreads abroad, Moments of Blessings Church also supports the Equator Faith Mission, which provides mission trips and outreach to Haiti, Jamaica, the West Indies and parts of Africa.

In 2011, the Moments of Blessings Church relocated to our new north campus location at 378 W. Fallbrook Avenue in the Riverpark Area of Fresno. The Lord has increased our territory and we are bringing the ministry of deliverance, hope, restoration and holiness to a new generation.
